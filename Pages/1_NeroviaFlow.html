💧 The Problem

Hidden leaks, invisible bills

Hotels lose 20‑40% of purchased water to leaks behind walls, faulty valves, and running toilets. No real‑time visibility. Facility managers only discover leaks after floods or when the bill arrives.

Morocco's water crisis

Water availability dropped 75% in one generation – from 2,560 m³ per person in 1960 to 606 m³ today. By 2030, absolute scarcity (<500 m³). Tariffs increased 40% last decade. Every drop counts.

Globally, 346 billion litres of treated water are lost every day – enough to end water poverty for millions. NeroviaFlow stops the waste where it starts: inside buildings.

⚙️ Our Solution

Non‑invasive, intelligent monitoring

We attach a clamp‑on ultrasonic flow meter externally to the main water pipe – no cutting, no plumber. A pressure sensor screws into an existing test valve. Both connect to a battery‑powered GSM gateway.

✅ 1‑hour installation · ✅ Works in pits without WiFi · ✅ 30‑day baseline learning

Dual‑sensor leak detection

During the night window (1‑4 AM), the system compares flow and pressure to the learned baseline. A leak is confirmed only when flow exceeds a threshold AND pressure drops simultaneously over several consecutive readings – zero false alarms.

📱 Instant SMS alert to facility manager. 💻 Live dashboard, monthly reports, optional ESG reporting.

🔊 Optional acoustic sensor (upgrade)

For very small leaks that generate noise but minimal flow/pressure change, an acoustic vibration sensor can be added as a third confirmation channel – further improving detection reliability.

📡 Technology & Hardware

Clamp‑on ultrasonic flow meter

Non‑invasive, attaches to any pipe (DN15‑DN50). Measures flow with ±1‑2% accuracy. 4‑20mA or pulse output.

Industrial pressure sensor

0‑10 bar, 4‑20mA output, 1/4″ BSP thread – screws directly into existing test valve. Detects pressure drops that confirm leaks.

GSM + battery gateway

ESP32 microcontroller, SIM800L GSM module, 18650 Li‑ion battery (30+ days autonomy). IP68 enclosure for pits/basements.
