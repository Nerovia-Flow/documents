🛡️ Insurance & legal protections

Professional liability insurance

Coverage up to 2,000,000 MAD per claim (AtlantaSanad). Protects against water damage claims from faulty installation or missed leaks.

Product liability insurance

Included in hardware supplier agreements (components). Additional policy for NeroviaFlow‑branded enclosure: 1,000,000 MAD.

Cyber insurance

Covers data breach response and legal defence (500,000 MAD). Active after first paid client.

Key‑person life insurance

2,000,000 MAD per founder (to be taken after sleeping partner funding).

🔒 Trade secret & collapse protocol protections

Trade secret (666.html)

Core algorithmic essence (attractor baseline, cross‑correlation thresholds, acoustic fingerprint, state machine counters) stored offline, accessible only to founders. Prevents replication even after patent expiry.

Collapse protocol (999.html)

Remote hardware kill‑switch, cloud data annihilation, legal IP demolition, physical asset erasure. Activated only upon hostile acquisition, system breach, or founders' incapacitation. Two‑factor Yubikey + dead‑man phrase.
